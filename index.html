Background
The Story Behind
The Automation

Before specializing in automation, I worked in process-heavy support roles including data entry, data validation, customer service, freelance admin support, and medical billing. Those roles trained me to work with accuracy, follow-through, and repetitive business tasks that need to be done right.

Working inside those workflows gave me a clear view of where businesses lose time — manual updates, repetitive emails, scattered information, delayed follow-ups, and operational tasks that depend too much on human effort. I did that work myself, so I understand the day-to-day pressure behind it.

That experience led me into automation with n8n. Today I build practical workflow systems that help businesses reduce manual work, improve response time, and keep operations organized — from lead handling and notifications to AI-assisted workflows and knowledge-based systems.

My advantage is simple: I do not build automation from theory alone — I build it from real operations experience.
